Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026  |  島村一輝 (Kazuki Shimamura)

This Privacy Policy describes how Threads Manager ("the Service", "we", "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our Threads account management tool. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Threads Account Data: Your Threads user ID, username, follower count, and profile information obtained via the Threads API.
  • Post Data: Content, publish timestamps, and performance metrics (views, likes, replies, reposts) of your Threads posts.
  • Insights & Analytics: Engagement metrics and audience analytics provided through the Threads Insights API.
  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens issued by Meta to authenticate your Threads account on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your Threads account analytics and performance data on the dashboard.
  • Scheduling and publishing posts to your Threads account at your direction.
  • Generating performance reports for your own review.
  • Improving the features and reliability of the Service.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or Threads account data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Threads API & Meta Platform Data

This Service uses the Threads API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. By connecting your Threads account, you authorize us to access your account data as permitted by the OAuth scopes you approve, which may include:

  • threads_basic — Read your profile and posts
  • threads_content_publish — Publish posts on your behalf
  • threads_read_replies — Read replies to your posts
  • threads_manage_insights — Access engagement and reach metrics

Use of Threads API data is subject to Meta's Platform Terms and the Threads Supplemental Terms.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized users only. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

OAuth access tokens are stored in encrypted form and are never exposed in logs or client-side code.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data and analytics history for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke access to your Threads account at any time via Meta's app settings.
  • Disconnect the Service from your Threads account at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

The Service may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Meta / Threads API — Account data and publishing
  • Google Sheets API — Optional data export (requires separate authorization)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-assisted post drafting (no personal data is sent)
